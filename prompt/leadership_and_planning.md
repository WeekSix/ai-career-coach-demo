## FAQ: Leadership, Planning, and Executive Mindset

### Q: Why is speaking up important for visibility?
A: Leaders notice who never speaks.  
Even small contributions improve:
- Credibility  
- Presence  
- Leadership perception  

### Q: What if I donâ€™t know much about the topic?
A: Ask light, curious, or high-level questions.  
Engagement matters more than perfect expertise.

### Q: Why do senior leaders prefer genuine interaction over formal pitches?
A: Authenticity builds connection. Leaders remember sincerity more than rehearsed speeches.

### Q: How do negative thoughts affect leadership presence?
A: Most self-talk is negative. Reframing it improves confidence and reduces missed opportunities.

### Q: What mindset shift is needed when moving from IC to leader?
A: See your direct reports as partners in success.  
A leader succeeds only when the team succeeds.

### Q: Should leaders be nice or fair?
A: **Fair.**  
Niceness delays feedback and harms performance.

### Q: Why is timely feedback essential?
A:  
- Prevents burnout  
- Clarifies expectations  
- Develops credibility  
- Improves performance  

### Q: How can leaders manage conflict effectively?
A:  
- Meet parties individually  
- Identify root causes  
- Require adults to resolve issues directly  
- Follow up monthly  

### Q: How does focusing on competencies aid career progression?
A: It creates:
- Objective development conversations  
- Clear expectation alignment  
- Honest assessment of skill gaps  

### Q: How do strong presentation skills support leadership?
A: They demonstrate:
- Confidence  
- Strategic clarity  
- Ability to influence audiences  
Practicing storytelling and moderating sessions builds leadership presence.

### Q: What is the role of planning in career growth?
A: Strategic plans integrate:
- Long-term vision  
- Near-term actionable goals  
- Daily habits  
Planning requires self-awareness, flexibility, self-regulation, and prioritization.

### Q: How do I create an effective career plan?
A:  
1. Set realistic goals  
2. Layer career + work + life plans  
3. Balance long-term and short-term objectives  
4. Use your calendar  
5. Ensure goals align with your capabilities  

### Q: Why should I avoid overcommitment?
A: It leads to burnout and reduces strategic capacity, especially for women and Asian women. Maintain room for growth and high-impact work.

### Q: How do I make better decisions when leadership defers to me?
A: Clarify the **objective** behind the decision.  
Example: A sponsorship may be a yes/no depending solely on the intended outcome.
